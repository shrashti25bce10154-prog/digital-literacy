Project Overview
This portfolio serves as a comprehensive toolkit designed to establish a safe, professional, and productive digital environment for the student community. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the primary objective is to lead by example, providing peers with the resources and frameworks necessary to excel in a digital-first professional landscape.

Folder Structure and Contents
1. Task 1: Presentation (/task-1-presentation)
Contents: A strategic pitch designed for the faculty committee.

Focus: Mission statement and the three pillars of digital literacy: Professional Branding, Ethical Communication, and Digital Safety.

2. Task 2: Digital Portfolio (/task-2-portfolio)
Contents: Optimized LinkedIn profile documentation and strategic reflection notes.

Focus: Establishing a professional digital presence and utilizing keyword optimization for industry visibility.

3. Task 3: Professional Platforms (/task-3-platforms)
Contents: Evidence of professional engagement and activity on LinkedIn.

Focus: Demonstrating the transition from a passive user to an active, value-adding contributor within professional networks.

4. Task 5: Email Etiquette (/task-4-email-etiquette)
Contents: Formal email templates and a professional "Netiquette" checklist.

Focus: Standardizing formal communication protocols for interactions with faculty and industry professionals.

5. Task 5: Cybercrime and Security (/task-5-cybercrime)
Contents: Phishing case study and a comprehensive cyber-hygiene checklist.

Focus: Protecting the student body from prevalent digital threats and securing academic information.
